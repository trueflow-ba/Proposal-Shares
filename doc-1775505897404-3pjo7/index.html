Executive Summary for Fishouse Pro

Fishouse Pro, led by Jack Snyder, is a growing restaurant known for its fresh seafood and exceptional service. However, as the restaurant's popularity grows, so does the volume of phone orders, often overwhelming the staff. With an increasing number of missed calls due to the busy schedule in a fast-paced kitchen environment, managing phone inquiries has become a significant challenge. Currently, Fishouse Pro relies on Clarity for their phone systems but needs an advanced solution to ensure every customer is attended to—without overburdening the team.

To address these challenges, our proposed solution introduces an AI-driven inbound call assistant designed to seamlessly integrate with Clarity and handle Fishouse Pro's phone orders. This system ensures no call goes unanswered, reduces staff distractions during peak hours, and delivers a consistent and professional customer experience. By automating routine inquiries and order-taking, your team can stay focused on what they do best: creating unforgettable dining experiences.

Our engagement will focus on three key pillars: delivering a tailored AI solution for restaurant-specific needs, ensuring seamless integration with Clarity, and providing ongoing support to maximize efficiency and reliability.

Restaurant-Focused Innovation — Our AI system is tailored specifically for high-volume restaurants like Fishouse Pro, ensuring it understands common phrases, menu items, and customer preferences unique to your seafood orders.
Seamless Clarity Integration — To prevent disruption to your operations, our solution will integrate directly with your existing Clarity phone system, enabling smooth transitions between AI and human interactions.
Reliable Ongoing Support — We provide continuous monitoring, updates, and support so that the AI assistant evolves with your business needs and remains operational under all circumstances.
